Homework 8 was to aid or refute an argument made at the "Talking Economics" event.

The panel were discussing how the pandemic could have affected children's education and development, and the implications for the future of the economy. I decided to chart the Education Index of a country against its GNI per capita, to see how education affects the economy. There is a clear correlation between the two. So if young people in the UK have had a worse education due to the pandemic, this could reduce GNI per capita in the future.
